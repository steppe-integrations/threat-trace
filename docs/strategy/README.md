- 05 (window pressure-test) — sets urgency calendar
- 08 (naming) — gates any external launch

**Vertical-specific (run after 01 confirms standalone is viable):**
- 02 (AML) and 03 (radiology) in parallel

**Strategic positioning (run before significant capital or time commits):**
- 04 (anti-thesis), 06 (OSS), 07 (acquirers)

**Execution (run alongside vertical research, not after):**
- 09 (first client) and 10 (third-party audit)

## Reading mode

These are working briefs, not finished plans. Treat each as a scoped task you could hand to an analyst, a partner, or a research subagent — or work yourself in 2–4 hours. Update them in place as you learn; they'll go stale fast.
